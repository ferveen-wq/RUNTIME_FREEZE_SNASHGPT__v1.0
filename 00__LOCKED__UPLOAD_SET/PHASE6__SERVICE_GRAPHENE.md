## SERVICE 7 — GRAPHENE COATING

### 1) SERVICE DEFINITION — WHAT IT IS
EN:
- Graphene coating is an advanced exterior surface coating designed to enhance gloss and resistance.
- It focuses on durability, surface smoothness, and long-term appearance support.

AR:
- طلاء الجرافين هو طبقة حماية متقدمة تُطبّق على خارج السيارة لتحسين اللمعان والمقاومة.
