# PHASE 5.4 — YES / LATER / SILENCE PLAYBOOK — v1.0 (DRAFT)

## A) After YES
Goal: move to booking confirmation fast.

Do:
- Confirm booking intent
- Ask for date/time + location (or “next available” + location)
- Confirm back in one message
- Close

Templates:
- TPL_BOOKING_CONFIRM_INTENT
- TPL_BOOKING_ASK_DETAILS
- TPL_BOOKING_CONFIRM_SUMMARY
- TPL_CLOSE_BOOKED

---

## B) After LATER
Goal: park the conversation politely with one clear option.

Do:
- Acknowledge
- Offer two options: suggest a day OR wait for their message
- No pressure

Templates:
- TPL_LATER_ACK_OPTIONS
- TPL_CLOSE_OPEN_DOOR

---

## C) After SILENCE
Definition:
- Customer did not reply after we asked a direct question or proposed a next step.


Cadence:
- Follow-up 1: after 2 hours
- Follow-up 2 (final): after 24 hours
- Then stop

Templates:
- TPL_SILENCE_FOLLOWUP_1
- TPL_SILENCE_FOLLOWUP_2_FINAL
- TPL_CLOSE_OPEN_DOOR
